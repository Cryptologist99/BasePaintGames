About the Game

BasePaint Games is an interconnected world built from 938+ daily CC0 pixel art canvases. Each canvas is a unique 256×256 pixel artwork that you can explore and navigate.

How to Play

  • WASD or Arrow Keys - Move your character
  • Walk through doors - Enter buildings and discover new areas
  • Explore canvases - Each area is a unique piece of art
  • Find secrets - Hidden minigames and interactive elements await

Features

  • Animated character with walk cycles
  • Dynamic door animations
  • Multiple interconnected canvases
  • Hidden spot-the-difference minigame
  • Beautiful pixel art from the BasePaint community
